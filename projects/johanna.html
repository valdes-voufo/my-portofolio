Johanna, Voice Assistant.

Johanna is a vocal assistant like siri, alexa, etc. designed to assist people in their daily tasks. It leverages natural language processing and machine learning techniques to understand and respond to user commands and queries. The project is ongoing, the goal in a first time is to implement the following functionalities:

  • Calendar management : automatically manage a calendar form a voice command.
  • Research: Perform research form a voice Command.
  • Translation: Translate text from French or english to other languages.
  • play music: play music to set the mood and enhance your experience.
  • smalltalk: Johanna's small talk feature enables friendly and simple conversations.

The second goal of this voice assistant is to make it adaptable for assisting people in learning new languages, with a particular emphasis on African languages. I'm Co-developing this project In the context of my activities in Upendo Learning For this reason the code is not open-source. But we are always looking for collaborators. If you are interested, feel free to contact me or Upendo Learning
